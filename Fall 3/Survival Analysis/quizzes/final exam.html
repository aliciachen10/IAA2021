If you were to plot the survival probability function for the above 10 customers, would your survival function reach zero? Why or why not?

The survival function would not reach zero when plotting the survival probability function for the above 10 customers. This is because some of the observations were censored, so we do not actually know what happens to them. They are considered at risk, but when they are censored they aren't contributing to the risk (numerator of the survival function). Question 13

Complete
4.00 points out of 4.00

Question text

Name one advantage and one disadvantage of a Cox regression model compared to an AFT model.

Cox advantage: No distribution assumptions (AFT you NEED a distribution assumption)

Cox disadvantage: Inability to predict beyond the data since there is no distribution assumptions being made. AFT can predict beyond the given data since there is a distribution assumption.

Question 22

4.00 points out of 4.00

Question text

In the competing risks framework we assume independence when we censor other types of events. What does this mean?

Assuming independence when we censor other types of events in the competing risks framework means that regardless of if an observation is censored or not, they have the same risk of having the event - it does not matter why they were censored.
